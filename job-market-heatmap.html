WHAT YOU'RE LOOKING AT

This map shows the health of each state's job market using 4 metrics from the BLS Job Openings & Labor Turnover Survey (JOLTS), December 2025.

Hiring Rate — new hires as % of total employment. Higher = more active hiring.
Job Openings — unfilled positions as % of employment. Higher = more demand for workers.
Layoffs Rate — involuntary separations as % of employment. Lower = more job security.
Quits Rate — voluntary resignations as % of employment. Higher = workers feel confident enough to leave.

As of Dec 2025, the US hiring rate sits near its lowest point since the 2008 financial crisis — the "K-shaped economy" in action.
